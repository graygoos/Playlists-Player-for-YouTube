We may collect the following types of information:

Disclosure of Information

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except as necessary to:

Data Security

We implement the following security measures to protect your sensitive data:

While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, no method is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
