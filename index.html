Welcome to Playlists Player for YouTube

Playlists Player for YouTube is designed to enhance your YouTube experience by providing a seamless way to view, manage, and play your playlists. Whether you're a casual viewer or a content creator, our app offers a range of features to customize and enjoy your music and video collections.

Purpose of the App

The primary purpose of Playlists Player for YouTube is to offer an intuitive interface for managing and playing YouTube playlists. It leverages the YouTube Data API v3 to provide both free and premium features, catering to different user needs. This app aims to simplify playlist navigation, enhance playback control, and provide advanced management options, all while adhering to YouTube's API Terms of Service.

Key Features

  • Free Features:
    • View and list all your YouTube playlists.
    • Play videos sequentially with basic controls (play, pause, skip).
    • Display video details like title, description, and thumbnails.
    • Search within playlists and bookmark favorites.
    • View channel information.
  • Premium Features (Available with Subscription):
    • Advanced playback options like shuffle, loop, and speed control.
    • Mini-player/Picture-in-Picture mode.
    • Create, edit, and reorder playlists.
    • View video statistics and app-specific watch history.
    • Advanced filtering, smart recommendations, and custom themes.
    • Interact with video comments.
